Non-Profit Exemptions

OpenGov is not for profit and is organized under section 501(c)3 of the U.S. Internal Revenue Code. That means we must comply with certain U.S. Government policies helpfully explained here by the U.S. Internal Revenue Service and here by Wikipedians. One policy is a prohibition on advocating for or against candidates for public office. We will make a good faith effort to ensure content on OpenGov websites is compliant.

Please email questions, comments or concerns about OpenGov compliance to sayhello@opengovfoundation.org.
